5.4 Public Sector Pensions
The level of additional revenue expenditure that will be required during 2025-26 is expected
to be around £40 million with further revenue expenditure being required each year into the
future. This expenditure is included within the 2025-26 budget and the indicative financial plan
as presented and does not represent an additional funding gap following previous reforms
made to the schemes.
